The Problem

Leads Are Slipping Through Every Day

In real estate, timing is everything. Manual processes cost you closings.

Slow Lead Response

The average agent takes 3+ hours to respond to a new lead. Research shows that responding within 5 minutes makes you 21x more likely to qualify the lead. Every minute of delay is money left on the table.

Showing Coordination Chaos

Scheduling showings across multiple properties, buyer availability, and listing agent access is a full-time job. Back-and-forth texts and calls eat hours that should go toward closing.

Follow-Up Gaps

Most leads are not ready to buy today. They need consistent, relevant follow-up over weeks or months. Manual nurturing fails at scale. Leads go cold and list with someone who stayed top of mind.
