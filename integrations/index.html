Integrations

We Plug Into Your Entire Stack

No rip and replace. Augentic AI connects to the tools you already use: your CRM, phone system, calendar, email platform, helpdesk, and everything in between. If it has an API, we integrate with it.

How It Works

Three Ways We Connect

01

Native Integrations

Direct connections to major platforms via their official APIs. Pre-built, tested, and production-ready. Most integrations are live within 24 hours of deployment.

02

Webhook and Automation

For tools that support webhooks, Zapier, or Make, we connect your AI agents through event-driven triggers. Real-time data flow without custom development.

03

Custom API Integration

For proprietary systems, legacy software, or anything with an API, we build custom connectors. Your AI agents work with your stack, not around it.
