FAQ

Common Questions

What is included in setup?

Every plan includes full setup at no additional cost. This covers discovery, workflow design, AI agent configuration, system integration, testing, and launch. We handle the entire deployment. There are no setup fees or onboarding charges.

How does billing work?

Billing is monthly with no long-term contracts required. You can cancel at any time with 30 days notice. We believe our work should earn your business every month, not lock you into a commitment you cannot exit.

Can I switch plans?

Yes. You can upgrade or adjust your plan at any time. If your needs grow, we scale your AI workforce to match. If you want to start with Starter and move to Growth after seeing results, the transition is seamless.

How long does setup take?

Starter deployments are typically live within 5 to 10 business days. Growth deployments take 2 to 3 weeks. Enterprise timelines depend on scope, but we prioritize speed across every engagement. You will not wait months for results.
